1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Account info: Email address, username (via Google Sign-In or direct sign-up)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Providing diary storage and emotion analysis

일기 저장, 감정 분석, 친구 공유, 알림 전송 및 서비스 개선을 위해 정보를 사용합니다.

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data in the following cases:

All personal data and diary entries are deleted when you delete your account. You can request account deletion in the app settings.

5. Security

All data is transmitted via encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored securely on our servers.

Cattopia is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
